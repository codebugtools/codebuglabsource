.. codelab:: Connect as Mass Storage (USB drive)

    #. Disconnect CodeBug Connect from all power sources.

    #. Press and hold the centre of Joystick A in while plugging CodeBug Connect into your computer's USB port. Keep it held until `USB` scrolls across in yellow. 
    
    #. CodeBug Connect will show a yellow file icon when it is ready to receive files.
